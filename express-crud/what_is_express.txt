Express is a minimal nodejs framework, a higher level of abstraction.
Basically it's a higher level of abstraction.
Express contains a very robust set of features : 
COMPLEX ROUTING
EASIER HANDLING OF REQUESTS AND RESPONSES
MIDDLEWARE
SERVER-SIDE RENDERING...

Express allows for rapid development of nodejs applications : we dont have to re-invent the whell.

Express makes it easier to organize our app into the MVC architecture.
